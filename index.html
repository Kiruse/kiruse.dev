About

Hi! I'm Kiruse, a Germany-based generalist developer for hire. I've been coding since my teen years: it's fun and jogs my noggin. I love working at night because historically, that's when I have my peace and quiet to fully concentrate and get creative.

While have I dabbled with many different techs, I have found a particular interest in full stack (both web & mobile), machine learning, blockchain, and game dev - although the latter is more of a hobby.

Over the years I have toyed with many different languages, though my primary languages are Python, JavaScript, and Julia. While I'm always open to learning new tech, my usual stack consists of React (Native) or Vue, Node.js, MySQL or SQLite. More recently, I ventured into the lands of Ethereum and IPFS. I'm looking to deepen my expertise with TensorFlow/Keras.
